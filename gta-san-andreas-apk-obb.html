How GTA San Andreas Redefined Open-World Gaming

Before GTA San Andreas, open-world games had limitations. The worlds were smaller, the mechanics simpler, and the stories less ambitious. Rockstar Games changed everything with a game that wasn’t just a crime saga but a full-blown cultural phenomenon.

1. A Massive Map with Three Iconic Cities

2. The RPG-Like Character System
