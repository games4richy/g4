The Legacy of Resident Evil 4

How Resident Evil 4 Changed the Genre

Unlike previous Resident Evil games, which emphasized tank controls and puzzle-solving, Resident Evil 4 introduced a dynamic camera system that allowed players to aim more precisely. This innovation influenced countless third-person shooters that followed.

The Game’s Impact on the Resident Evil Series

The success of Resident Evil 4 led to a shift in how future games in the franchise were developed. While some fans preferred the traditional survival horror elements, the game’s action-oriented approach helped the series reach a broader audience.
